Welcome to NovaSight

A Dashboard system designed to display the predictive result of student performance in Bataan Peninsula State University - Main Campus

This system leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to predict student performance with a high degree of accuracy and insight. By analyzing large volumes of historical academic data such as grades, GWA, irregular, regular, male-female ratio, drop, inc the system is able to uncover hidden relationships, trends, and indicators that may not be immediately visible through traditional evaluation methods. These predictive models continuously learn and improve over time, allowing them to adapt to new data and provide increasingly reliable forecasts of a student’s academic trajectory. With access to these data-driven insights, academic administrators can make more informed decisions about their educational policy, such as improving study strategies, or academic prevention.
